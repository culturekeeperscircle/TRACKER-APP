#### 2. **litigation-2026-cv-imls-defunding**
- **Date:** March 27, 2026 (Filed)
- **Case:** American Library Association, AFSCME, Public Libraries Association v. Trump
- **Court:** U.S. District Court, District of Columbia
- **Title:** Challenge to DOGE Elimination of the Institute of Museum and Library Services
- **Status:** PROTECTIVE (TRO Granted April 1)

**Key Legal Claims:**
1. Violation of Administrative Procedure Act (no notice-and-comment rulemaking)
2. Violation of Congressional appropriations authority (Congress appropriated $313M, EO reduced to $6M)
3. Impoundment of appropriated funds (unconstitutional)
4. Violation of Museum and Library Services Act (statutory mandate)

**Plaintiffs:** American Library Association (140,000 members), Public Libraries Association, AFSCME, 40+ state library associations

**Key Issue:** Congressional appropriations cannot be unilaterally reduced by executive order. IMLS serves 123,000 libraries and 35,000 museums nationwide.
